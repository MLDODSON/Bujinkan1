The Saint Louis Bujinkan Dojo is a private, non-commercial martial arts school dedicated to the study of Japanese warrior thought and culture. We are a fully licensed dojo through the Bujinkan Shidoshi-kai (instructors guild based in Noda-shi, Japan) and have proudly served the Saint Louis area since 2005 as a center for learning the martial arts of the ninja and samurai. Our training is non-competitive and is focused on the skills, strategies, and philosophies refined by war during the feudal Japan era. We accept new students year round and are committed to providing the highest quality and most accurate representation of the training that can be found in Japan.
